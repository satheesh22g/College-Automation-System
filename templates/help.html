Using the Search Feature

The Education Search Hub allows you to search for student information using natural language queries. You can search by:

  • Roll Number: "161CS027" or similar format
  • Student Name: "John Doe" or "Doe"
  • Commands: "show my attendance", "show my marks", etc.

Counselor Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view student attendance? +
  • attendance of counseling students
  • attendance greater than (any number, e.g., 75)
  • attendance less than (any number, e.g., 75)
  • attendance of (specific roll number, e.g., 164G1A0593)
  • show my counsel students
How do I check student marks? +
  • marks of students with less than (%), e.g., 60 percentage
  • marks of students with greater than (%), e.g., 60 percentage
  • marks of counseling students
  • marks of (Roll Number, e.g., 164G1A0593)
  • how many failed students
  • how many all clear students in my counseling
  • how many all clear students
How do I view student profiles? +
  • Profile of (Roll Number, e.g., 164G1A0593)
  • Profile of counseling students

HOD Frequently Asked Questions

How do I view department attendance? +
  • attendance less than (%), e.g., attendance less than 75 of year 2
  • attendance greater than (%), e.g., attendance greater than 75 of year 3
  • attendance of (specific student, e.g., 164G1A0593)
  • attendance of (year) year students, e.g., attendance of 2 year students
How do I analyze student marks? +
  • marks with less than 60 percentage of 3 year
  • marks with greater than 60 percentage of 3 year
  • marks of 2 year (for specific year)
  • marks of (roll number, e.g., 164G1A0593)
  • how many failed students in (year, e.g., 2nd year)
How do I view student profiles? +
  • profile of (roll number, e.g., 164G1A0593)

Helpful Tips

  • The system uses natural language processing, so your queries are auto-corrected
  • You can use keywords like "greater than", "less than", "of", "in" etc.
  • Year numbers are represented as 1, 2, 3, 4 for 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th year
  • If you don't find what you're looking for, try using the Education Search Hub with different keywords
  • Contact system administrator if you encounter any issues
